> Everything shown below is built with an iteration of a compute-shader based particle system I built from the ground up in Unity over the last two years.

> It has many features, including external forces, vector fields, and optional collision detection. I typically build new features for projects based on creative and integrate them into the larger system.

> It was used in VR in "Zikr" and "The Day The World Changed", at Sundance and Tribeca respectively.

> These projects are representative of my approach to using particle systems as a core element of immersive experiences, and my desire to understand natural systems through computing.

> Enjoy :)
